Shrimp

Shrimp are all born male, then mature into females.They go through 16 different stages of life, from egg to adult.The longest shrimp on record was 16 inches long.There are well over 128 species of shrimp.A mantis shrimp has claws that can break aquarium glass with one incredibly fast and powerful swing.

  • Scientific Name: Caridea
  • Average Length: 1.5 to 3 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 1 to 7 years
  • Habitat: Rivers, Oceans & Lakes.
